How It All Began

The Beginning

It was something neither of us expected—that fate would intertwine our paths. We were just friends, casual acquaintances who interacted online but not much beyond the digital realm. Little did we know that those simple exchanges would blossom into something beautiful.

The Realization

One day, like the sun breaking through clouds, I realized you were the person I wanted in my life. Not just as a friend, but as someone special who could share in life's journey with me. It was a quiet revelation, but one that changed everything.

November 1st

We met for the first time on 1st November at the Shopping Centre in Kenyatta University. The world seemed to pause in that moment, and I could feel how special you were to me. Your smile, your voice—everything about you made that day unforgettable.

"Sometimes the heart sees what is invisible to the eye."

Our Journey Together

It has been 164 days since that first meeting, and now we come together again today. Each day has been a treasure, a gift that I cherish deeply.

I'll never forget how shy you were that day, how you faced the wall so others couldn't see you. That endearing moment made me realize how precious and unique you are. Your genuine nature, your authentic self—these are the qualities that drew me to you.

"In all the world, there is no heart for me like yours."

My Promise To You

Sharlyn, you have transformed ordinary days into extraordinary memories. You've filled my life with joy, laughter, and meaning in ways I never thought possible.

I promise to be there for you through every sunrise and sunset. To cherish the beautiful moments and stand by you through the challenges. I promise to listen when you need to be heard, to comfort when you need support, and to celebrate when you achieve your dreams.

Most importantly, I promise to love you genuinely and completely, today and always.

"Love is not about how many days, months, or years you have been together. Love is about how much you love each other every single day."
